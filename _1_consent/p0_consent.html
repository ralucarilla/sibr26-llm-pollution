Thank you for your interest in this study. Please read the declaration of consent carefully.

Rationale for this study
This study was designed by researchers at the University of Basel, Switzerland, and the Max Planck Institute for Human Development, Germany, to better understand people's views, concerns, and opportunities about Artificial Intelligence (AI).

Inclusion criteria and risks
To participate in this study, you must be at least 18 years old, proficient in English, and agree to complete the study with focus and attention. There are no known risks associated with participation.

Compensation and benefits of the research
The survey will take approximately 5 minutes. For participation, you will be compensated with a flat fee of £0.75. Additionally, you will benefit indirectly by helping us advance our understanding of people's views on AI.

Collection of data
Your participation in this study is voluntary. You have the right to withdraw from the study at any time without explanation. However, if you withdraw, you will not receive compensation. Your data will be treated anonymously and stored securely on a server at the Max Planck Institute for Human Development. It will be used solely for scientific research and may be shared with academic researchers for non-profit research purposes.
